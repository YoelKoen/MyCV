About Zombie Knock Out

Zombie Knock Out is a 3D comedy roguelike where you find yourself in a bizarre boxing tournament. The opponents are zombies, and they're all very proud of their fighting prowess. If you win, you get to leave with your brain intact. If you lose, well, they get to enjoy a delicious snack. The twist is that while they are honorable in a 1-on-1 fight, they are also incredibly gullible.

The "Cheating" System

How to Win a "Fair" Fight

The zombies respect the rules of a fair fight, but their pride and limited brainpower can be exploited. You can buy weapons like a gun or sword and, through dialogue choices, convince the zombie referee and your opponent that it's a "legal" move. This allows you to cheat your way through each round and progress deeper into the tournament.

Roguelike Progression

Each run is a new attempt to win the tournament. You'll need to figure out which weapons and dialogue options work best against different types of zombies. Since you can't be expected to get it right on the first try, the game's roguelike structure allows for continuous learning and adaptation with each new run.

My Next Steps

I'm currently designing the different classes of zombies from the slow and simple to the surprisingly clever to see how each one can be persuaded. I'm also focusing on creating the absurd dialogue trees that will justify using a sword in a boxing match.
